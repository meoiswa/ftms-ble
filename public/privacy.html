Privacy Policy

FTMS Dashboard  ·  Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

FTMS Dashboard is a free, open-source web application that connects to Bluetooth fitness machines and lets you record and review workout sessions. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your data.

The short version: all your data stays on your device. We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data we collect

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts are loaded.
  • No cookies are set by the application.
  • No data is sent to any server operated by this project.

Data stored on your device

Workout sessions (timestamps, duration, speed, power, heart rate, cadence, distance, and calories, as reported by your fitness machine) are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Google Drive sync.

User preferences (theme, per-device field settings) are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data is not shared with anyone.

Google Drive (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, the application requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope. This scope grants the application access only to files that it creates — it cannot read, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive.

The application uses this permission solely to:

  • Upload your recorded workout sessions as JSON files to a folder named FTMS Dashboard in your Drive.
  • Download those same files to sync sessions across devices or browsers.
  • Delete a session file when you delete the corresponding session in the app.

Your Google account credentials are never stored by this application. The OAuth access token provided by Google is held in memory only and is discarded when you sign out or close the page.

Google's own privacy practices are described in the Google Privacy Policy.

Bluetooth

The application uses the Web Bluetooth API to communicate with your fitness machine. Bluetooth data is processed locally in your browser and is never transmitted over the internet.

Third-party services

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used. The only external network requests the application makes are to the Google Drive REST API, and only when you are signed in.

Children's privacy

This application does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

This project is open source. If you have questions or concerns, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
